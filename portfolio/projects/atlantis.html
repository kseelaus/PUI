Atlantis Hub Display

Iterative design to produce a clean information display for travel by plane, train, and ferry

Challenge

Our team was tasked with designing a digital display for the Atlantis Transportation Hub in the San Juan Islands. The display would manage data updates for ferry, plane, and train data, providing visitors with the necessary information to travel seamlessly between the several islands.

Solution

Through several progressive iterations, we designed a clean display that highlighed only the most important information. We incorperated animation and an interactive feature to increase the value for our (made-up) stakeholders.

Context

Interaction Design Studio, Carnegie Mellon University, Fall 2018
