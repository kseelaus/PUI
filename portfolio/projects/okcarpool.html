OkCarpool

Generative research and design on improving student trasportation

Challenge

To implement user research methods to help the Graduate Student Assembly (GSA) pinpoint an area to improve student transportation options

Solution

Through ethnographic, quantitative, and usability testing of available resources, we found that many students do not have transportation options that they consider convenient. We designed and prototyped a system whereby an app, tentatively called OkCarpool, could be used to coordinate ride sharing between students who have cars and those who need rides.

Context

User-Centered Research and Evaluation, Carnegie Mellon University, Fall 2018
